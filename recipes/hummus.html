Hummus

Hummus is a delicious spread or dip made from chickpeas, tahini, lemon, and spices. Itâ€™s commonly eaten in the Middle East and the Mediterranean.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Tahini
  • Fresh lemon juice
  • Fresh garlic
  • Ground Cumin
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Find your food processor.
  2. Add Tahini and lemon juice.
  3. Let the food processor run for about a minute.
  4. Now add ingredients as you will.
    Make sure you are running the food processor for about a minute after every ingredient.
  5. Drizzle some cold water during the last minutes of running the food processor.
  6. You're done!!!
